# User Guide

This guide explains how a trader or analyst should use each dashboard page.

## Overview

Use this page for the daily desk read.

Primary questions:

- Which markets moved most over the selected window?
- Are fuel prices transmitting into Japan power prices?
- Is JEPX system price above or below coal/gas SRMC?
- What does the forward curve monitor imply about nearby pricing?

Default focus starts from February 2026 where available.

## Power Market

Use this page for JEPX price and liquidity monitoring.

Primary questions:

- How are system, Tokyo, Kansai, and intraday prices moving?
- Is Tokyo/Kansai basis widening?
- Is intraday liquidity improving or deteriorating?
- Are day-ahead and intraday markets converging or diverging?
- Is weather stress aligned with area power moves?

## Fuel & Dispatch

Use this page for fuel competitiveness and dispatch economics.

Primary questions:

- Is LNG or coal more competitive on SRMC?
- Is JKM dislocated versus JCC-linked LNG?
- Is power underreacting to fuel-price moves?
- Is USDJPY amplifying imported fuel cost?

## Forward Curves

Use this page for market expectations.

Primary questions:

- Is the curve in contango or backwardation?
- Where are quarterly strips priced?
- How have recent curves shifted?
- Are power futures and physical baseload references telling the same story?
- Does the uploaded/vendor curve pass schema and tenor checks?

## Weather & Seasonality

Use this page for demand regime context.

Primary questions:

- Are Tokyo/Kansai temperatures entering cooling or heating stress?
- Which months/weeks tend to be structurally stronger?
- Is the current period aligned with summer or winter peak patterns?

## Trading Signals

Use this page for rule-based desk prompts.

Primary questions:

- Which market relationships are stretched?
- What is the rationale and invalidation for each signal?
- Are fuel, power, weather, curve, or market-structure signals reinforcing each other?
- Are public news items relevant to nuclear, solar, grid, fuel, or JEPX market operation?

Signals are monitoring prompts, not trade recommendations.

## Supply Mix

Use this page for generation structure context.

Primary questions:

- How does Tokyo/Kansai generation share differ by fuel type?
- Is thermal dependence rising or falling?
- Is nuclear/solar changing the regional supply profile?

Bundled data is synthetic unless replaced by validated upload or future public/vendor pipeline.

## Market Structure

Use this page for ex-post JEPX aggregate bidding-curve analysis.

Primary questions:

- How much depth sits around estimated clearing?
- Which delivery blocks are thin or shock-sensitive?
- Did sell-side depth or buy-side depth drive the curve shift?
- Which time-of-day period tightened or loosened most?
- Is Tokyo or Kansai the thinner stack?

Do not interpret this page as participant-level offer behavior. It uses public aggregate curves.
